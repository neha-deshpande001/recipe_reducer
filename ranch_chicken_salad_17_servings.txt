Yield: 17 servings 

Ranch Chicken Salad 

  Prep Time: 15 mins 
  Cook Time: 0 mins 
  Total Time: 15 mins 
  Course: Lunch, Salad 
  Cuisine: American 

  This light and easy chicken salad recipe made with leftover rotisserie chicken breast meat and my homemade ranch dressing. Great for lunch and meal prep! 


  Ingredients 

      * 2.125 cup 1% buttermilk 
      * 12.75 tablespoons mayonnaise 
      * 8.5 tablespoon fresh finely chopped chives 
      * 2.125 teaspoon kosher salt 
      * 1.0625 teaspoon garlic powder 
      * 1.0625 teaspoon onion powder 
      * 1.0625 teaspoon dried parsley 
      * 1.0625 teaspoon dried basil 
      * fresh black pepper (to taste) 
      * 8.5 cups shredded boneless chicken breast (from rotisserie chicken or leftover) 


  Instructions 

    * In a medium bowl combine the buttermilk, mayo, chives, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, parsley, basil and black pepper and mix. 
    * Add the shredded chicken and mix well. Refrigerate until ready to eat.
